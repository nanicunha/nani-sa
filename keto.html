01  ·  At Its Core

At Its Core

At its heart, the keto diet is about changing how the body gets energy. Instead of using carbohydrates, the body starts burning fat.

I chose this topic because many people are interested in this diet and want to understand how it works.

02  ·  Definition
📖

Definition

The keto diet is a low-carbohydrate, high-fat diet that helps the body enter a state called ketosis.

We are not focusing only on the diet itself, but also on how it affects people's health and daily life.
03  ·  Reducing Carbohydrates
🍞

Reducing Carbohydrates

One important characteristic of the keto diet is reducing carbohydrates.

This process can be difficult for some people because many common foods like bread, pasta, and sugar are limited.

04  ·  Possible Benefits
⚡

Possible Benefits

More often than not, people who follow the keto diet lose weight and feel more energetic.

However, the results can vary depending on the person.

05  ·  Balance Matters
⚖️

Balance Matters

In and of itself, the keto diet is not necessarily unhealthy — but it requires balance and proper planning.

06  ·  Conclusion
🎯

Conclusion

Fundamentally, the keto diet shows how changing what we eat can affect how our bodies work.

"Sometimes, the problem isn't the body — it's what we've been feeding it."
